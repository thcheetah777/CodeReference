Let's learn some HTML! Below are some tags to keep in mind

1. <!DOCTYPE html>/represents that the website is HTML based!

2. <html lang="en" dir="ltr >/represents the left to right movement of the text!

3. <head >/data of the data(size,date,etc)!

4. <body >/contains the content of the doc!

5. <title >/contains the title of the doc!

6. <link >/relationship between current and external doc!
